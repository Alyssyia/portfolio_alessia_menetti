Virtual_Production .Underground()

NABA developed within its campus in Milan one of the first Virtual Set in Italy, presented by the BA in Creative Technologies and composed by a 40 sqm ledwall, a motion analysis system, and a physical set. The installment is born to explore how the technology works and how the hybridisation of techniques and languages of game development is changing the world of film production, with a national and international perspective. The Virtual Set was the scenario for the filming of two short movies. I were in charge of modeling and texturing the Underground virtual scene with two other students.

Technical partnership with Twinone
Clothes: UNICORE
Backstage photos: Stefania Carbonara

_Rolleiflex{}

The classic 3D model of a Rolleiflex. Realized as a school project which then I corrected and completed on my own.

I’ve used a combination between Automotive Materials and materials form Quixel Bridge for texturing in Unreal Engine.

Homeless.Home()

Restyling of an old school project entirely made in Maya with two other 3D modeler. After correcting the old models I imported and texturized them in Unreal Engine. Here I tried to understand how particle systems work and find the correct workflow for using them.

Meph[ala]

Personal project modeled in Zbrush.

The subject represented is a tribute to the character of Mephala in Skyrim, where she is the Daedric Princes of Obfuscation and Secrecy. Her minons are known as Spider Daedra.

bye.bye()

Sound reactive 3D visual built in Touch Designer. It was my final project for Multimedia Installation course I was attending in NABA.

I drew the pixel art in Aseprite and then imported it in Touch Designer and assigned a box for every pixel to make it work.

Watch it on youtube, music byrosencr4ntz

William\'s_Shelter

Thesis project made in Unreal Engine 4 with one fellow student.

It took us 6 months, characters are made in Metahuman and animations are recorded with the Perception Neuron suit.

This short movie is part of a bigger project. We have written the full story of an adventure game, and this is meant to be the cinematic introduction of it.

About_Me

Freelance 3D and Digital Artist born and raised in Bologna, actually living in Milan. Attending the BA in Creative Technology at NABA, New Academy of Fine Arts.

Since I was a kid I always loved to represent the world around me on paper (or walls, for my mum’s happiness) with all sorts of mediums I could find.

When I turned 12 I started to gain interest in videogames and their ability to carry you in new dimensions. Since I wanted to learn how to do the same, I attended the Arts High School where I learned how to work with different techniques and mediums and how to observe the world with a discriminating look.

During my last year at school I found out about the existence of 3D modeling, and decided to attend NABA in Creative Technologies and learn how to work in this industry.
